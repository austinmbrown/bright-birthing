Bright Birthing Monthly Village Circle

  • When: Sunday, September 27 - 4:00pm - 6:00pm

As part of our September Village Circle we will hold a gathering to honor spirit babies.

Near the autumnal equinox, recognizing the balance of darkness and light: a thoughtful, contemplative time to gather and remember those lost to miscarriage, stillbirth and abortion.

Our gathering will include poetry, prose, prayers and anything you hope to share. Contributions are welcome including candles, symbolic objects.

& our inspiration, the Spirit Babies community in the Bay Area: http://www.spiritbabies.org/

Bright Birthing Monthly Village Circle

  • When: Sunday, August 30 - 4:00pm - 6:00pm

Join us for our monthly gathering: sharing, camaraderie, snacks and tea. We'll have a talk by Wendy Vigdor-Hess (author of Sweetness without Sugar) on introducing solids and making baby food at home.

All current and former Bright Birthing families are welcome, as well as pregnant folks, those considering pregnancy, folks with babes in arms or birth-workers.

"The mothers come from near and far. They have a variety of birth plans and care providers. We are there to shower them with village attention and support and to bathe them in a sense of belonging and being cherished. The one inspiration and encouragement we echo over and over in our stories, on our walks, in our drumming and deliciously in our feasting is this: Trust your Instincts. Follow what makes sense to you. Do it your way. Take your time. Find a place of peace to make decisions. Speak your Truth. Find your Truth. Your body has all the right answers. No one has your knowing. Build your instincts. Trust your instincts," - Sister MorningStar

Bright Birthing Mothers' Day Yoga Picnic

Join us at Belmont Park on Mothers' day from 3-5 for a playtime picnic and 60 minute yoga practice led by Sara Zia. Bright Birthing will provide healthy snacks and refreshments and welcomes potluck contributions. Kids are welcome to practice and can tip toe out and head for the playground at any point. There is plenty of space for munchkins to run around, play and hang back with other adults while mamas and friends savor some time on their mat.

Class will begin at 3:15 with potluck reception to follow. We hope to see you and your loved ones at the park and on your mats on Mothers' day!

Message us on Facebook or email brightbirthing@gmail.com for questions
