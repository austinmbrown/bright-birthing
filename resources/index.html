Postpartum doula support

After birth everything is new and different. Postpartum doulas provide insight, experience, validation and education during the transition after pregnancy. The doulas of Month 10 also offer a weekly meeting at C'ville Coffee on Tuesdays at 12:30. Expectant and new moms are invited and the community is a very sweet one.

Photography

Whether you'd like to schedule a maternity photo shoot, capture precious newborn portraits or include a photographer in your labor, Meredith Coe does impeccable work. As a classmate at the Academy of Experiential Midwifery Education she's also a kindred spirit.
