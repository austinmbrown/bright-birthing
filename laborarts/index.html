Labor Arts

Visual and expressive arts can play a great role in supporting the inward journey of pregnancy and labor. The families I work with can incorporate any of my creative offerings into their service package, but I'm also happy to offer them separately to anyone. Contact me for fees or to ask any questions you may have.

Belly Blessing with Mendhi (henna artwork)

For a blessingway or baby shower, a belly blessing with henna serves as a sweet connecting and honoring time for belly and baby. Incorporating songs, prayers, readings, poetry and friends can make it a welcome celebration.

Mandalas

My painted mandalas are available framed for contemplative meditation or simply for decoration and can be custom-designed for color pallette and size.

Altars

Finally, I encourage my clients to choose treasured items or symbolic objects for creating a small, portable labor altar. This is a great way to bring the love and support from home with a laboring mom to her birth place, if it's elsewhere.
